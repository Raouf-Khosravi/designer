Café Interior Plan

Overview: Designed a functional and aesthetic café floor plan optimized for customer flow, seating capacity, and kitchen efficiency. Integrated commercial appliances, furniture blocks, and visual elements for a client-ready, professional interior layout.

Layout Features

• Dining Area:

Multiple round and square tables with 4 chairs each — designed for optimized seating and traffic flow.

• Counter Zone:

L-shaped counter with detailed kitchen appliances and work areas behind it (grill, sink, cooler).

• Service Flow:

Clear customer path from entry to counter and then seating.

• Decoration:

Planters along the front window add realism and ambiance.

Notable Details

• Consistent use of furniture blocks and hatch patterns.

• Rotated angle of flooring grid for aesthetic variation.

• Annotations for equipment and workflow spacing, like counter depth and clearance zones.

Skills Demonstrated

• Commercial interior planning with ergonomic and spatial logic.

• Detailed kitchen layout with respect to staff movement and customer interaction.

• Professional presentation style using color-coded elements and scalable furniture blocks.
