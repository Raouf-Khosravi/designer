Residential House Design

Overview: Developed a full set of architectural 2D drawings for a two-story residential home, including facades, floor plans, sections, and site layout. Emphasized spatial clarity, human scale, and detailed layer management for presentation and permitting purposes.

Key Elements

• Multiple Facades:

Showcasing all four external views of the residential house with consistent detailing of windows, door placements, and exterior finish.

• Section Views:

Reveal internal room heights, wall thicknesses, stair locations, and even garage elevation. Human and car figures provide scale.

• Floor Plans:

   -   Ground Floor:

Includes main entrance, living room, kitchen, and possibly a guest room with access to outdoor patio.

   -   First Floor:

Likely holds private spaces like bedrooms and bathrooms with a central hallway connecting them.

• Site/General Plan:--

Shows boundary lines, outdoor vegetation, driveway, and path layout—situating the house within its property context.

Highlight Features

• Clear differentiation of architectural layers using color codes (walls, doors, furniture).

• Realistic scale figures in section views for enhanced understanding.

• Landscaping and property layout included—suitable for presentation and permits.

Skills Demonstrated

• Complete 2D architectural set including facades, sections, and floor plans.

• Application of line weights and layer organization.

• Use of AutoCAD layout space and scaling techniques.
