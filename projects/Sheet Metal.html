Sheet Metal Design Projects

Overview:These designs showcase practical applications of sheet metal tools with a focus on real-world fabrication, laser cutting, and CNC bending. Each model is fully unfoldable and ready for manufacturing, demonstrating awareness of tolerances, bend reliefs, and cutout standards.

Electronic Enclosure with Fan & Ventilation Cutouts

• Tools Used:Base Flange, Edge Flange, Cut-Extrude, Vent Design, Flat Pattern

• Overview:Designed a ventilated electronics box using standard sheet metal thickness. Included fan cutouts, hexagonal cable holes, and optimized vents for airflow. Created with bend reliefs and flat pattern ready for manufacturing.

• Applications:Power supply units, control boxes, electronic housings.

Precision Sheet Metal Hinge – Multi-Bend Geometry

• Tools Used:Sketched Bend, Hem, Jog, Tab, Edge Flange

• Overview:Modeled a fully functional hinge assembly with tubular folds and multiple bend lines. Special focus on sheet thickness, alignment during bending, and real-world manufacturing constraints.

• Applications:Sheet metal enclosures, door mechanisms, foldable assemblies.
