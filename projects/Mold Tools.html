Mold Tools & Core-Cavity Design

Overview:These mold designs demonstrate an advanced understanding of core/cavity creation, shut-off surface definition, and tooling split techniques. I’ve focused on multi-cavity efficiency, draft analysis, and manufacturable geometry suitable for real injection molding processes.

LEGO Mold – Interlocking Part Mold Design

• Tools Used:Parting Line, Shut-Off Surfaces, Tooling Split

• Overview:Designed a two-part mold for a LEGO-compatible brick, ensuring tight tolerance for snap-fit interlocking. The mold features core pins, cylindrical bosses, and uniform draft angles to ensure easy demolding.

• Applications:Toy manufacturing, high-precision plastic molding, consumer products.

Ice Cube Tray Mold – Multi-Cavity Plastic Tray

• Tools Used:Intersect, Core/Cavity, Parting Surfaces

• Overview:Modeled a dual-part mold for producing a 24-cavity ice cube tray. Focus was on achieving uniform wall thickness, controlled taper angles, and clean parting surfaces for efficient plastic injection.

• Applications:Household item manufacturing, plastic consumer products, thermoforming pre-designs.

Molded Chair Design – Complex Hollow Part Tooling

• Tools Used:Tooling Split, Shut-Off Surfaces, Parting Line

• Overview:Created a mold for a miniature chair with a hollow-back design. The mold includes internal ribs and sharp geometric transitions, requiring accurate shut-off surfaces and smart parting strategies.

• Applications:Furniture modeling, toy design, ergonomic component molds.

Citrus Juicer Mold – Circular and Textured Surface Design

• Tools Used:Core/Cavity, Knit Surface, Shut-Off Surface

• Overview:Developed a mold for a manual citrus juicer with ribbed conical surfaces and peripheral juice filters. The geometry included circular symmetry and undercut regions, resolved using surface stitching and shut-off patches.

• Applications:Kitchen tools, complex surface molds, ergonomic product tooling.
