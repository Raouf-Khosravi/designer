Weldments & 3D Structural Design

Overview:These structural designs showcase proficiency in 3D sketching, profile-based modeling, and weldment standards. Each frame or structure is optimized for strength, manufacturability, and modular fabrication, aligned with real-world industrial applications.

Industrial Steel Bed Frame – Rectangular Tube Assembly

• Tools Used:Structural Member, Trim/Extend, Gusset Plates

• Overview:A heavy-duty steel bed frame constructed using rectangular hollow sections. The model incorporates real-world weldment standards and is optimized for strength and manufacturability.

• Applications: Heavy furniture, welded frames, load-bearing designs.

3D Truss Structure – Parametric Spatial Frame

• Tools Used:3D Sketch, Weldments, Structural Members (Angle & Tube), Trim/Extend

• Overview:A complex spatial truss modeled using 3D Sketch and Weldment tools. Designed for maximum rigidity and minimal weight, showcasing proficiency in three-dimensional structural path creation and realistic member connections.

• Applications:Event stages, modular scaffolding, architectural frameworks.

Wall-Mounted Pull-Up Bar – Tubular Weldment Design

• Tools Used: Structural Member, End Trim, Gusset, Weldment Cut List

• Overview:Designed a robust pull-up bar mounted to a wall, using circular tubing profiles and reinforcement gussets for strength. Emphasis was placed on weldability, strength-to-weight ratio, and modular fabrication.

• Applications:Fitness equipment, welded structures, strength training gear.
